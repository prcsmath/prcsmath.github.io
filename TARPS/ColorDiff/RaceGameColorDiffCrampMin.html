Color Differences

In this challenge, you will be given pairs of almost identical grids which differ in a few locations. Your task is to identify these locations by position. Once you give the correct wersand to a problem, you will advance to the next problem. After you solve all 15 problems, screenshot the finish page and upload it to your command channel.

WARNING: The problems are randomized, so if you refresh the page, you will have to start all over!
